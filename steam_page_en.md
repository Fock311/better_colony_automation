## ❌ Still manually managing dozens of planets?

Mid to late game in Stellaris often turns into a micromanagement nightmare:

- Manually building on every single planet  
- Vanilla automation building randomly and even ruining your economy  
- Planet specialization requires tedious manual demolition and rebuilding  

As your empire grows to dozens or even hundreds of planets,  
this quickly becomes overwhelming.

**Better Colony Automation** aims to solve this:

**Eliminate repetitive actions while keeping full control over your empire’s development.**

## ✅ What does this mod do?

✔ Automated construction **based on actual needs** (not random building)  
✔ Automatically removes unsuitable buildings, districts, and specializations for smooth planet transitions  
✔ Smart resource control to prevent automation from crashing your economy  
✔ Highly configurable, but works out-of-the-box  
✔ Intuitive UI for planning and adjustments  

This mod is designed to **reduce workload, not replace player decisions.**  
👉 **You set the direction — the system handles the execution.**

## Quick Guide

1. **Enable automation**: Turn on Planet Automation and configure options in the planet interface  
2. **Planning & district setup**: Use the new UI to configure mixed planning and district construction  
3. **Global settings**: Configure global behavior in Policies (e.g. mineral reserve for auto-construction)

This mod improves almost all vanilla automation options, including pop assembly, housing, rare resources, crime management, etc. You can safely leave these options on by default.

## ⚠️ Compatibility

**It is strongly recommended to place this mod at the end of your load order**

- This mod primarily modifies vanilla automation logic and should not conflict with mods that do not touch automation  
- Only supports vanilla buildings and districts (no support for modded buildings/districts for now)  
- Not compatible with mods that add additional district specializations or new district types  
- Compatible with mods that increase building slots  

This mod is compatible with Ultimate Automation,  
but **must be loaded after it** to override its changes.
